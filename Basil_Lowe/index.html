Research

My objective is to is to build reliable intelligent systems for interpreting visual data. This is a broad goal that encompasses various mathematical and computer science topics, including partial differentiation equations (PDEs), inverse problems, image generation and restoration, deep-leaning models et. al, with application in areas like vision-text LLM, visual perception, medical image analysis and so on. Influenced by recent research experiences, I am currently most interested in investigating scene recovery, single-image super-resolution (SISR), and influence function for reliable vision data. Moreover, based on past research experience, I am also willing to explore the vision-language models, vision perception for robots, design reliable algorithms and interpretable LLMs for image processing and generation.
